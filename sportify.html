Sportify is an Android application whose goal is to connect users who are interested in engaging in outdoor activities with other users who share the same interest. Unlike other competing Android-applications like Meet-Up or social networking websites like Facebook, Sportify intends to simply the communication process between users and allow users of the application to join and host outdoor activities with ease.
