Autograder

Automating Homework assignments and electronic queue

- Integrating Moss (Measure of software similarity) into Autograder

- Email notification functionality for students requesting different tasks on Autograder

- Displaying student and tutor reviews on personal accounts

Project components include:

- Automatic grading of computer science lab assignments using a distributed test runner with detailed diff reports for errors in student code.

- Electronic "help queue" that aids tutor involvement in cse labs and allows instructors to analyze hotspots and downtime in tutoring hours.

- Online management of assignment credit and grading including assignment checkoffs, integrated student-grader followup chains, and regrade requests.

Overseen by Faculty Mentor Gary Gillespie.

Sportify

An application to connect people with the same physical activity interests

Contribution:

- Implemented the base code of the project, list view of all the activities, and the filtration of the app.

- I was recognized twice for the most contributing team member by my teammates.

Sportify is an Android application whose goal is to connect users who are interested in engaging in outdoor activities with other users who share the same interest. Unlike other competing Android-applications like Meet-Up or social networking websites like Facebook, Sportify intends to simply the communication process between users and allow users of the application to join and host outdoor activities with ease.
