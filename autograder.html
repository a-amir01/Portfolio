Autograder

Student run project at UCSD

Contribution:

- Integrating Moss (Measure of software similarity) into Autograder

- Email notification functionality for students requesting different tasks on Autograder

- Displaying student and tutor reviews on personal accounts

Project components include:

- Automatic grading of computer science lab assignments using a distributed test runner with detailed diff reports for errors in student code.

- Electronic "help queue" that aids tutor involvement in cse labs and allows instructors to analyze hotspots and downtime in tutoring hours.

- Online management of assignment credit and grading including assignment checkoffs, integrated student-grader followup chains, and regrade requests.

Overseen by Faculty Mentor Gary Gillespie.
